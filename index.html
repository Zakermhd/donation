My Dear Colleague
I am writing to you today with a heavy heart to share some personal news about Mr. Zaker Mohammed, who has faithfully served as my car driver and family caretaker for the past five years. Recently, Zaker received a devastating diagnosis of Chronic Myeloid Leukemia, a type of blood cancer. He comes from a family with limited financial resources, and the cost of the best available treatment is unfortunately beyond their means. Zaker is a dedicated and kind individual who has become an integral part of our family. His positive attitude and unwavering work ethic have earned him our deepest respect and appreciation. We are determined to help him fight this battle and secure the best possible treatment. To this end, I am reaching out to my network to request your support in raising funds for Zaker's treatment. Any contribution, big or small, will make a significant difference in his journey towards recovery. For your convenience, I have created this dedicated website that outlines Zaker's situation and provides secure donation options through his UPI ID, mobile number, and bank account . We understand that these are challenging times, and any support you can offer, whether financial or emotional, will be deeply appreciated by Zaker and our family. Thank you for your time and consideration.
Sincerely,
